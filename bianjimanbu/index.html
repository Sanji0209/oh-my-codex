Responsible data use

We use platform data only for authorized business operations.

To manage advertising performance, we may use TikTok Marketing API or other approved advertising platform APIs after account-owner authorization. The data is used for internal dashboards, campaign reporting, creative evaluation, budget pacing, and e-commerce growth analysis.

We do not scrape TikTok, collect unauthorized user data, resell platform data, or share advertising data with unauthorized third parties.
