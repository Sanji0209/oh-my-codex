Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Overview

Shenzhen Bianjimanbu Technology Co., Ltd. respects privacy and data protection requirements. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect data when operating cross-border e-commerce and advertising analytics tools.

2. Data We Process

When authorized by an advertiser account owner, we may process advertising account data such as:

  • Advertiser account IDs and account-level information.
  • Campaign, ad group, ad, and creative information.
  • Reporting metrics including spend, impressions, clicks, CTR, CPC, CPM, conversions, CPA, and ROAS.
  • Date-level, campaign-level, product-level, and creative-level performance data.

3. How We Use Data

We use authorized advertising data only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Internal reporting and marketing dashboards.
  • Campaign performance analysis and budget pacing.
  • Creative performance evaluation and product research.
  • Advertising optimization for our own or authorized advertiser accounts.

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not scrape TikTok or other platforms.
  • We do not collect unauthorized user data.
  • We do not sell, resell, or broker platform data.
  • We do not share advertising data with unauthorized third parties.
  • We do not access advertiser accounts without authorization.

5. Data Sharing

We may share internal reports only with authorized employees, contractors, or advertiser account owners who need access for campaign management and business operations. We do not disclose platform data to unauthorized parties.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including access controls, account authorization management, and limited internal access based on business needs.

7. Data Retention

We retain advertising reporting data only for as long as necessary for internal analytics, reporting, compliance, and business operations, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@bianjimanbu.com
